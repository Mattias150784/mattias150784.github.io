The Random Item mod is a fun and unique mod that is compatible with any mod and vanilla items! Every player on the server receives a random item at regular intervals, keeping the gameplay exciting and unpredictable. Whether you're using items from vanilla Minecraft or your favorite mods, the Random Item mod ensures that every item in the game has a chance to be gifted to players even the creative items such as Bedrock!!!
